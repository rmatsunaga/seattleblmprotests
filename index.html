Black Lives Matter Protests in Seattle

Click me for more information on the black lives matter movement!

Descriptions

This is a webmap that is meant to show the various protests occurring in Seattle 2020 by the Black Lives Matter Movement. Basic functions include clicking on a point of protest and see when the protest occurred as well as seeing how it fits into our greater Seattle community by having outlines of certain areas that are heavily affected.
